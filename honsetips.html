Fun Facts About Horses

Honse Care Tips

Honse require special care, unlike any regular horse. Follow these steps to ensure your honse lives its best chaotic life!

1. Feed Your Honse Wisely

Your honse has refined tastes. Always offer a variety of snacks, especially glittery carrots and enchanted apples.

2. Honse Needs Free Range

Never confine a honse! A honse needs the open skies, endless fields, and maybe a portal to another dimension. Let them roam free and occasionally float if they feel like it.

3. Dance with Your Honse

At least once a month, engage in a spontaneous dance-off with your honse. This strengthens your bond and recharges their spirit energy.

4. Listen to Their Secrets

Honse have an ancient wisdom. Listen carefully when they neigh at the stars—it’s likely important, or at least amusing.

5. Never Try to Tame a Honse

A honse’s spirit cannot be tamed. Don’t even try. Just let the chaos flow and enjoy the ride.
